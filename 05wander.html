  Wander algorithm will return point which can be approach by Seek. The sole method creates a circle in front of vehicle and after taking random point on circle it will start to slightly modify it. Thanks to those small steps vehicle will be allowed to move ahead quite smoothly and randomly.
  The orange vector pointing out of the center of Vehicle is visualization of the desiredVelocity and the violet one shows current velocity vector. The radius of circle and vector pointing to that circle can be modified.
  If you wish to freeze animation you can also press a spacebar on your keyboard.

wander(length = 30, radius = 25) {
  var lengthVector = this.velocity.normalize();
  lengthVector = lengthVector.multiply(length);
  lengthVector = this.position.add(lengthVector);
  this.theta = this.theta + ((Math.random() * 15) - (15 * 0.5));
  var radiusVector = new Vector(Math.cos(this.theta) * radius,
  Math.sin(this.theta) * radius);
  radiusVector = lengthVector.add(radiusVector);
  return radiusVector;
}
